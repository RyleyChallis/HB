Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

At Hellenic Bakery, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information and data.

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide to us through our "Send Message" contact form, such as your name, email address, and phone number.

We use your information specifically to respond to your inquiries, process cake orders, or provide information about our menu items.

3. Data Security

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. We use standard security measures to protect your data locally.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request a copy of the information we hold about you or ask us to delete any personal data we have collected through our contact form.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us in-store or with our contact form.

6. Cookies

We may use "cookies" to enhance your experience and gather general visitor statistics. You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser settings.

Our website contains links to social media platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these external sites.
