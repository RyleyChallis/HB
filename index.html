Our Specialties

Handcrafted daily in the heart of Seven Dials, using traditional family recipes passed down through generations.

Authentic Greek Pies

Experience the true taste of Greece with our handmade village pies. From our signature Spanakopita (Spinach & Feta) to golden Tiropita (Cheese Pie), every item is baked daily using traditional family recipes and the finest Mediterranean ingredients.

Artisan Greek Sweets

Indulge in our extensive range of handcrafted desserts. We specialise in honey-soaked Baklava, crispy Mille-feuille, and rich Custard Nutella treats. Whether you’re looking for a single pastry or a mixed gift box, we have the perfect sweet for every occasion.

Traditional Coffee Bar

Brighton's home for authentic Greek coffee. Enjoy a refreshing Freddo Espresso, a classic Frappe, or a smooth Flat White. Pair your brew with our fresh morning bakes for the ultimate Seven Dials coffee break.

About Us

Here, you'll get to know where we source our ingredients and how our delicacies are crafted.

  • The Finest Imports

    We source our extra virgin olive oil directly from Kalamata and our honey from the mountains of Crete to ensure every bite is authentic to the culture.

  • Hand-Rolled Daily

    Our filo pastry isn't store-bought. It’s rolled by hand in our Seven Dials kitchen every morning at 4 AM to achieve that perfect crunch.

  • Heart of Brighton

    More than just a bakery, we are a gathering place for the Brighton community. We're proud to serve our neighbors the best Greek coffee in the city.

  • Never From Frozen

    If it’s on our shelf, it was baked today. We donate any leftovers to local Brighton food banks, ensuring nothing ever goes to waste.
