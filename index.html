Why Brighton Loves Us

Authentic Flavours

We offer traditional Greek recipes passed down through generations, going back centuries. Our authentic desserts are the closest you'll get to Greece in all of Brighton! Experience the true taste of Crete today!

Freshly Baked

To provide our wonderful customers with the best experience possible, we wake up long before opening time to prepare our dishes, ensuring the community receives the Hellenic tradition it deserves.

Our Warm Staff

Here at Hellenic Bakery, we treat all of our customers like family, like our own. No matter what day of the week it is, we'll always greet you with love and a sparkly smile!

Our Authentic Staples

Spanakopita

Handmade phyllo pastry filled with fresh spinach, tangy feta, and Mediterranean herbs.

Baklava

Layers of crisp pastry and crushed walnuts, finished with our signature honey-cinnamon syrup.

Sourdough

Slow-fermented for 24 hours to create a deep, crunchy crust and classic Cretan tang.
